About

WHOAMI?

I'm Sérgio Henrique, a Computer Engineering student at Universidade de Pernambuco (POLI), in Recife, Brazil.

Most of my time goes into testing backends and APIs, writing test automation, and programming microcontrollers (Arduino and ESP-32) for battle robots and home-made gadgets. The rest goes into coffee.

Besides studying, I work as a Quality Assurance Junior at BIODOC, making sure complex applications work as they should, and I build autonomous robots with my university's robotics team. You can read more about both on the Career and Projects pages.

Things I care about: software that works (and proving it doesn't), clean and well-documented code, hardware you can hold, teaching people to program and sites made by people, for people. I believe good solutions come from conscious decisions, not from doing it "the fastest way".

Warning: may contain traces of caffeine and late-night coding sessions.
